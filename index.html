About

AI for Computational Biology

LSAIL studies computational biology at the interface of life science and artificial intelligence, developing AI methods that connect molecular structures with biological context.

We develop AI methods that connect molecular structures with biological context, including phenotype, genotype, transcriptomics, morphology, and other multimodal life science data.

Our work spans broad computational biology, with major applications in drug discovery, toxicity and safety prediction, and biology-aware molecular modeling.
